  • What are the installation requirements and processes for this ERP software?

    The installation process for our ERP software is straightforward and typically takes less than a week. We come with a dedicated implementation team who will guide you through the installation process and ensure that the software is configured to meet the specific needs of your business. We provide all installation requirements include a compatible hardware device, internet connection and a specific version of operating system.

    Our robust HR management capabilities that include employee records management, payroll processing, benefits administration, and compliance tracking.
